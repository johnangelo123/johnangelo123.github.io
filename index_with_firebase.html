Our Story

We are the proud creators of the best coffee experience!

Founded by a passionate group of computer engineering students, Janedy G. Panesa, John Angelo Oquiana, Radvic Prudente, and Princess Constantino, we created this coffee shop with a vision to bring high-quality, freshly brewed coffee to our community. We are dedicated to providing our customers with the freshest organic beans and a welcoming space to enjoy their favorite brews.

Our Vision

As owners, our vision is to share our love for coffee with everyone while promoting sustainability and organic farming practices. We aim to create an inviting atmosphere where people can relax, recharge, and experience the best coffee brewed with passion.
